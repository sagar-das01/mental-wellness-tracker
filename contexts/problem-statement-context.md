## Main problem statement

### Mental Wellness Tracker

#### Description

Build a generative AI powered solution which helps students monitor and improve their mental well-being during high-stakes board exams and competitive entrance exams.

Toughest of Indian exams like NEET, JEE, JEE-Advanced, CUET, CAT, GATE, UPSC


#### Goal
Students preparing for these exams or milestones often face severe stress, burnout and self-doubt. 
We are planning to create a simple yet engaging tool that leverages GenAI to analyse open ended daily journaling and mood logs, the system should be able to uncover hidden stress triggers and emptional patterns which standard conventional trackers miss. 

The solution should use conversational AI to provide hyper-personalized feedback, contextual wellness support, such as, real time stress detection, coping strategies, adaptive mindfulness exercises and motivational encouragement- safely acting as an empathetic, always available digital companion through their personal academic journey.
